# Privacy Policy

**Last updated:** February 2026  
**Applicable to:** TonGPT bot and related services (the "Service")

## 1. Who we are

TonGPT is a Telegram bot and associated backend services that provide TON ecosystem information, AI-powered analysis, and subscription features. This policy describes how we collect, use, and protect your information.

## 2. Data we collect

We collect only what is necessary to operate the Service:

- **Telegram account data:** Your Telegram user ID, username, and first/last name (as provided by Telegram) when you interact with the bot.
- **Wallet address:** If you link a TON wallet via TON Connect, we store the public wallet address associated with your Telegram account. Blockchain addresses are public by nature.
- **Usage data:** Commands you use, subscription tier, and chat messages sent to the AI in order to provide context and improve responses. Chat history may be stored for a limited time for service operation.
- **Payment-related data:** For Telegram Stars or TON payments we process payment identifiers and plan choices to activate subscriptions. We do not store full payment card or private key data.

We do **not** require or collect email, phone number, or real-world identity for standard use.

## 3. How we use your data

- To provide and personalize the Service (e.g., AI replies, subscription features, wallet linking).
- To process payments and manage subscriptions.
- To enforce rate limits and prevent abuse.
- To comply with applicable law and protect the security of the Service.

We do not sell your personal data. We do not use your data for advertising profiling.

## 4. Legal basis (EEA/UK)

Where applicable (e.g., if you are in the EEA or UK):

- **Contract:** Processing necessary to provide the Service you requested.
- **Legitimate interests:** Security, abuse prevention, and service improvement.
- **Consent:** Where we rely on consent, you may withdraw it at any time (e.g., by stopping use and requesting deletion — see Section 7).

## 5. Data sharing and transfers

- **Telegram:** We operate within Telegram’s platform; Telegram’s privacy policy applies to data handled by Telegram.
- **AI providers:** Messages you send to the AI may be processed by third-party AI providers (e.g., OpenRouter/OpenAI). Their privacy and data processing terms apply to that processing.
- **Infrastructure:** We may use cloud or hosting providers (e.g., for Redis, databases, or servers) that could be located outside your country. We require appropriate safeguards where legally required.

We do not share your data with advertisers or data brokers.

## 6. Retention

- **Account and subscription data:** Retained while your account is active and as needed for legal and operational purposes.
- **Chat and usage data:** Retained for a limited period necessary for service operation and then removed or anonymized in line with our retention policy.
- **Payment records:** Retained as required for accounting, dispute resolution, and legal compliance.

## 7. Your rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have the right to:

- **Access:** Request a copy of the personal data we hold about you (e.g., via `/export` in the bot where available).
- **Rectification:** Request correction of inaccurate data.
- **Erasure:** Request deletion of your data (e.g., via `/deletedata` in the bot where available).
- **Portability:** Receive your data in a structured, machine-readable format.
- **Restriction / object:** Restrict or object to certain processing.
- **Withdraw consent:** Where processing is based on consent.
- **Complain:** Lodge a complaint with a supervisory authority in your country.

To exercise these rights, use the in-bot commands above or contact us (e.g., via the support channel indicated in the bot). We will respond within a reasonable time as required by law.

## 8. Security

We use industry-standard measures to protect your data (e.g., encryption where appropriate, access controls, secure configuration). No system is completely secure; we will notify you and regulators where required in the event of a breach affecting your data.

## 9. Children

The Service is not directed at children under the age required by their jurisdiction (e.g., 13 in the US, 16 in the EEA where applicable). We do not knowingly collect data from such users.

## 10. Changes

We may update this policy from time to time. The "Last updated" date at the top will change, and we will notify you of material changes via the bot or Telegram channel where appropriate.

## 11. Contact

For privacy-related questions or to exercise your rights, contact the operator via the support channel indicated in the TonGPT bot (e.g., @TonGPT_Support or the link provided in the bot).
